About My Game

Gustav is your avergae cream cheese loving clown with a family who he loves and hates. one day gustav wakes up in his house to find his nephew Ned just sitting there. Normally this wouldn't be a problem but Ned has to get to school! unbeknownst to Gustavs knowledge Ned's father nico dropped him off to go run some errands without telling Gustav. Now it's up to Gustav to get Ned to school.
